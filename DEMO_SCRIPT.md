# SENTINEL AI — DEMO SCRIPT (90 seconds)

> Rehearse this out loud. Every line is timed. Every keystroke is marked.
> The story is: real crisis → real company → real alert → real action → real scale → real price.

---

## BEFORE YOU START

- Browser open to `localhost:5173/dashboard`
- Globe is auto-rotating, news ticker scrolling
- Hands off the keyboard for the first 10 seconds
- LIVE badge is green in the header

---

## [0:00–0:10] THE CRISIS — *Don't touch anything.*

> "Wednesday night, China blocked yttrium export licenses. By Thursday morning, prices had spiked 60%. Two US aerospace manufacturers had to halt production — they had no warning, no backup suppliers, and no plan."

*(Globe rotates slowly. Trade arcs glow. Vessels crawl. News ticker scrolls real headlines. Let the screen do the work.)*

> "Their competitor — Cascade Precision — kept building. Because they had Sentinel."

---

## [0:10–0:22] THE COMPANY — *Press `1`*

*(Globe resets to overview. All arcs, all facilities, all chokepoints visible.)*

> "Cascade Precision. $3.8 billion. They make titanium castings and avionics housings for Boeing and Lockheed Martin."

*(Trace the screen with your hand — left to right, Asia to Americas)*

> "Eight factories. Six countries. Nineteen vessels in transit right now. And $2.4 billion of their supply chain — 63% of their entire revenue — runs through four geopolitical chokepoints."

---

## [0:22–0:42] THE ALERT — *Press `2`*

*(Globe tilts 45° and flies to Taiwan. IntelPanel slides in from the right. Score animates up. Causal chain reveals step by step.)*

> "This morning, Sentinel flagged Taiwan. Risk score: 71 — and climbing."

*(Point at the causal chain items as you say them)*

> "PLA naval exercises expanding into the strait. A 180% spike in GDELT tension events. A $1.8 billion US arms package approved last week. Our LSTM model forecasts this crosses 78 — HIGH classification — within 60 days."

*(Point at the exposure box at the bottom of the panel)*

> "For Cascade, that's their Hsinchu chip packaging facility — $680 million a year, a hundred miles from the Taiwan Strait. Every avionics board they ship to Boeing goes through there."

---

## [0:42–0:55] THE ACTION — *Scroll the panel, then press `5`*

> "Most platforms stop at the alert. Sentinel gives you the action."

*(Point at the RECOMMENDED ACTIONS cards in the IntelPanel)*

> "Dual-source with Samsung Foundry in South Korea. Twelve million dollars to qualify a second supplier. The cost of NOT doing it? $680 million. That's a 56-to-1 return."

**Press `5`** — *(Actions page loads. $70M historical losses banner. KPI numbers animate. Hotspot cards with coverage bars fill in.)*

> "This is the full mitigation portfolio. Every threat. Every action. Prioritized by urgency. Each one has cost-to-act, cost-of-inaction, and ROI — so the CFO can sign off in one meeting."

---

## [0:55–1:12] THE SCALE — *Press `0`, then `3`, then `0`*

**Press `0`** — *(Flies back to full globe. Panels close.)*

> "We don't just do this for Taiwan. We do this for every country on Earth."

**Press `3`** — *(Flies to China. IntelPanel opens with rare earth alert.)*

> "China's yttrium export controls? Sentinel flagged that risk three weeks before the price spike. The recommendation was already in the queue — stockpile six months from Lynas in Australia. $3.2 million. The alternative was a $420 million production shutdown."

**Press `0`** — *(Back to full globe.)*

> "201 countries. Four ML models. 47 features per country. 107 data sources. 75 years of conflict history. Updated every fifteen minutes."

---

## [1:12–1:30] THE CLOSE — *Hands off. Let the globe spin.*

> "Right now, this kind of intelligence costs fifty million dollars a year. Palantir, Recorded Future — they sell to governments and Fortune 100s. The mid-market manufacturers who are MOST exposed to these risks? They get nothing."

> "Sentinel AI. Five hundred dollars a month. The geopolitical intelligence platform that pays for itself before the first alert."

*(Full globe. All arcs pulsing. Vessels moving. News ticker running. LIVE badge green.)*

## RECOVERY CHEAT SHEET

| Situation | Key | What happens |
|-----------|-----|-------------|
| Lost / need a reset | `0` | Always returns to safe globe overview |
| Panel blocking the view | `Esc` | Closes panel, globe stays |
| Judge asks about methodology | Click "Reports" in sidebar | Shows ML pipeline, accuracy stats, prediction log |
| Judge asks about AI | `Ctrl+K` | Opens chat — ask "What's driving Taiwan's risk?" |
| Judge asks about countries list | Click "Countries" in sidebar | Sortable table, 201 countries, all scores |
| Need to show Red Sea | `4` | Flies to Yemen, shows shipping risk |
